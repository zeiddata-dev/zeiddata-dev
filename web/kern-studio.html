The studio

Est. 2019

A small team,
on purpose.

Kern is deliberately small. Every project is led by the people who pitch it — no hand-offs, no account layer, no juniors learning on your budget.

We take on a handful of clients a year and stay with them for the long stretch: first identity, then the site, then the printed matter that follows. The work gets better when the relationship lasts.

We start by listening, we show fewer options and better reasons, and we ship files your next agency could pick up without calling us.
